#  Credit Card Financial Dashboard

##  Project Overview

This project is a **two-layer Credit Card Analytics solution** built using Power BI.

It integrates:

- Customer Analytics  
- Transaction Analytics  

###  Objective

- Who generates the highest revenue?
- What customer segments drive profitability?
- Which transaction behaviors contribute most to revenue?
- How do card categories perform across different segments?

---

#  Dashboard Architecture

## 1️⃣ Transaction Analytics Dashboard

###  KPIs
- Total Revenue  
- Total Interest Earned  
- Total Transaction Amount  
- Total Transaction Count  

###  Analysis Modules
- Revenue by Card Category  
- Revenue by Chip Usage (Swipe / Online / Chip)  
- Revenue by Expenditure Type  
- Quarterly Revenue Trends  
- Total Transactions vs Revenue Comparison  

###  Business Objective
Identify high-performing card types and revenue concentration areas.

---

## 2️⃣ Customer Analytics Dashboard

### KPIs
- Total Revenue  
- Total Interest  
- Total Income  
- Customer Satisfaction Score (CSS)  

### Analysis Modules
- Revenue by Age Group  
- Revenue by Income Group  
- Revenue by Education Level  
- Revenue by Job Category  
- Revenue by State  
- Gender-based Revenue Distribution  

###  Business Objective
Identify high-value customer segments and demographic revenue drivers.

---

# Key Insights

- High-income customers contribute the largest revenue share.
- Business professionals and white-collar segments are strong revenue drivers.
- Age group 40–60 shows significant financial activity.
- Blue and premium card categories dominate revenue.
- Transaction channels impact revenue patterns.
